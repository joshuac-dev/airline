Bombardier

Bombardier Aerospace is a Canadian aerospace manufacturer that designs, manufactures, and sells business jets, commercial aircraft, and regional airliners. Bombardier has been a significant player in the regional and business aviation markets, known for producing efficient aircraft for shorter routes.
