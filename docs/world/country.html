Country

A country is an area on the map that defines various things, such as nationality of people and airports, and the origin of airports to name a few.

All Countries and Information

The following includes a list of all in-game countries, which includes some not fully recognized countries, as well as territories.

Country Information

  • Total Population cover- Amount of passengers that are of the country's nationality
  • Income level- Average income of passengers, the lower the value, the lower the price tolerance is (and vise versa)
  • Openness- a value <7 will result in airports that have No International Connection, which will make passengers not take connecting flights through that airport (i.e. if a passenger wanted to go SEA-HGK via PVG, they would need to take two separate flights, SEA-PVG on one airline and PVG-HGK on a different airline. This does not apply to international-domestic (e.g. LHR-CAN-SHA can be taken on one airline)
