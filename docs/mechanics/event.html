Event

The in-game event system creates a temporary change depending on the event that is active. There is currently one event in the game so far.

Olympics

The four years of the Olympics.

The Olympics are an international sporting event that occurs every 4 years, where athletes from countries all around the globe compete for medals in a variety of sports. In-game, the Olympics happens once every 4 years, although preparations (for airlines) starts way before the planned week.

Year 1

Once the last week of the Olympics finishes, the voting for the next Olympic begins. A selection of six towns/cities that are located above the -45 or 45 latitude line from different countries will be voted using a ranked-choice system (where the first place you click will be your most preferred city, second one you click will be the next preferred city, and so on and so forth). Airports that are open to serve the Olympics must be within a 100km radius.

In order to vote, your reputation must be 40 or above. Otherwise, you will get a message saying you will be unable to vote.

Years 2 and 3

The winner from the vote from the previous year is announced, as well as which city was eliminated when. If a city was eliminated, it's votes go to the next-highest city that is still in the running (per person). If you voted (

These years are for preparation and planning. In this time, you can set up routes to the host city. However, you will not get any score from passengers transported at this time.

Year 4

This year you will be able to gain score. Score is based on the amount of passengers transported. If you reach your target score, you can claim a reward of $20,000,000 (or score, whichever one is higher), or a loyalty boost. Not every airline has the same target score.

Score is about 1 point per passenger directly transported, 1/2 point if you transport a passenger for one part of a leg (transfers), 1/3 for three transfers, etc. Just because you have a certain amount of passengers to the target city, you will not always get the same amount points in return

]

. In addition, note that each pax transported = 1 point, and route distance plays no role in determining the score, just the number of pax and the number of segments they travelled on.

If you surpass the score goal, you get the option of choosing one of the four rewards:

  • Cash $20,000,000 or $500*score (whichever one is higher)
  • Loyalty: +25 loyalty boost on airports in the 100km range of the host city for 4 years
  • Reputation: +10 reputation for a limited time
  • Delegates: Base of +3 delegates for 100% completion, and an additional delegate for each additional 100% of goal completed up to 400% for a maximum of 6 delegates (for 208 weeks, one full Olympics cycle)

Choose wisely!

Christmas

Around Christmas (usually beginning the first Saturday of December, until the first or second week of January), there is a mini-game that involves guessing where Santa is in the world, by clicking on airports. There is an option to enable snowflakes raining down across your screen as well

Rewards

You are able to choose one of the following:

  • Cash reward of $20,000,000 (Santa Claus is feeling generous! He is giving you $20,000,000 cash!)
  • Service quality boost of 5 for 8 weeks (Santa Claus offers you his pro tips on making your flights magical! Overall Airline Service Quality bonus +5) [ notes 5 ]
  • 3 loyalty in your HQ for 8 weeks (Santa Claus agrees to travel to your HQ and ho-ho-ho for 8 weeks in [HQ airport] Loyalty bonus 3 in your HQ airport!)
  • 10 loyalty for 8 weeks in the airport you found Santa in (Santa Claus is going to print your airline logo on all the gifts shipping for [Airport you found Santa in]! Loyalty bonus 10 in [Airport you found Santa] for 8 weeks!)
  • 5 reputation [ notes 6 ] for 8 weeks (Santa Claus will make an appearance on your TV commercial! Boosting your airline reputation by 5!) [ notes 5 ]
  • 3 delegates for 1 year/52 weeks (Santa Claus dispatches his elves to help you! 3 extra delegates for 52 weeks!)

Once you guess, correctly or not, you must wait until the mini-game resets. This reset is controlled by the dev, and usually is done once a week.
