V2.2

v2.2 is the latest game update, released Saturday, August 23, 2025. No world reset will be done for v2.2. Below is a list of changes:

Aviation Hub

Aviation hub

was introduced to incentivize making transit hubs out of small airports. Read more about how the charm is calculated and its benefits

here.

Reputation tier changes

Delegates are earned by reaching a certain amount of reputation. v2.2 would lower the reputation required to reach another tier and get 3 extra delegates, especially at lower levels.
