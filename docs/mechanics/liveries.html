Liveries

Liveries are assortments of colors, graphics, and identifiers (text) on an aircraft that are painted on an aircraft.

The game allows you to import a livery to represent your airline in the

office

menu.

Designs

You don't have to be overly creative to make a good looking livery, just have a play after watching the either two linked tutorials (or many others online), it can be very satisfying to make your own, or if you aren't up for it, get on AC'd discord/livery requests & ask for one of the talented members there if they can make one & they will happily do one for you.
