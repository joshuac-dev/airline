Menus

The game menus allow a player to access different areas of the game. To access them, move your mouse to the left edge of your screen under the header & left click to open.

The menu's in Airline Club

World Map

Shows the map with your routes and

bases

.

World map centered on Europe

Flights

Lists all your

routes

.

Flight menu

Country

Lists all the countries, their information, and the delegates you have sent to that country.

Country management panel

Airplane

Lets you buy

airplanes

, and shows their information.

Airplane menu

Office

Shows you all the information about your airline, income, delegates, championed airports and countries, delegate pool, and the rebuild/declare bankruptcy airline option.

Office menu

Bank

Gives you the option to take loans.

Bank menu

Oil

The history of fuel is shown, with an option to buy fuel contracts

Oil menu

Rivals

Gives you information about other airlines in the game. Airline with a bronze medal next to them are Patreons. Airlines with a star next to them are admins.

Rivals menu

Ranking

Lists the highest airlines in the following categories: weekly passengers, weekly passenger miles, highest weekly route profit,

reputation

, service quality, airport weekly passengers, and total flight routes.

Leaderboard

Alliance

Lists all the alliances in the game, allowing one to join or create one.

Alliance menu

Log

Shows alerts and game messages.

379.01x379.01px

Events

Shown information about events. Allows you to vote for a host city in the Olympics, and to claim rewards from the Olympics.

236.992x236.992px

Search

You can look up your own or competitor's flights to any airport. There is also an option to look at the changes on those routes. These options can be found by clicking on the options on the top of the search bar. It is also very handy for researching a route you wish to establish.
