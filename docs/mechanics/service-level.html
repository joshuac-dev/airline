Service Level

Service levels for each route are determined both by your overall service funding, which is set on the

office

page, and each individual route's service level star-rating. A route with a higher service level will incur higher costs under "Service Supplies" each

week

, but will increase the

Satisfaction Factor

of the passengers on that route.
