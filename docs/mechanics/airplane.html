Airplane

Airplanes are the method of transport the game is focused on. There are over 100 different models (135 to be exact) available in the game.

Airplane Menu

The airplane menu can be accessed by clicking the airplane icon from the left-hand list.

How fast an aircraft will fly (in km/h). Important for determining how many times the plane can fly on a given route, and for customer satisfaction. It also determines aircraft minutes.

Runway Requirement

The minimum distance the aircraft needs runway length at both the departure and arrival airport in a flight route in order for the plane to be operable. In V1, this used to be called airport scale, which was a rigid categorization system.

Manufacturer

The company (and country the company is from) that produces the aircraft. If your starting country is not on friendly terms with the manufacturer's country of origin, that model can not be purchased.

Category

An airplane can either be listed as light, regional, medium or large

Turnaround Time

Time it takes to service the airplane on the ground (e.g. empty PAX, refuel, clean etc.). Higher turnaround times may limit route frequency.

Other things you can do

Favorited

If you own at least 5% of the total aircraft of a specific model in circulation (not counting the planes that were replaced/sold, you can set it as a favorite. In doing so, you do get 25% off the delivery time, and some discount off the price. The discounts depend on the airplane's category (see here for exact

details

). Click the heart icon next to the aircraft model in the airplane detail section. You can only change it every 52 weeks.

Handy graph showing how often an airplane will have a delay/cancelation based on condition.

Repairing

When a plane's condition gets lower, there are more chances for

delays and cancellations

to occur. You can repair (called replace in the game) planes by going to the Airplane tab, clicking on the Base button of the aircraft model you want to repair, click on the plane you want to replace, then press the replace button (if you have the funds). The cost to renew is the the current cost of the plane minus its selling value.

Auto-renew

Going into the

office

will let you set a condition for your planes to be replaced at. Once a plane's condition goes under the percent you chose, the game will automatically replace it (assuming you have the funds).

Selling

To sell a plane, follow the same steps to replace a plane, but press the sell button in the last step. To calculate the selling value, use the following formula: current cost to buy a aircraft*(airplane condition)*.8*(any discounts, i.e. 25% off would be *.75). You will get the selling value back immediately, and your aircraft will be placed on the Used Aircraft market, and other airlines can buy them.
