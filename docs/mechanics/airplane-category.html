Airplane Category

Airplanes fall into different categories based on their capacity. There are two different kinds of categories, one used in the Hangar, and one that is used for determining different information.

Definition

Hangar

The hangar uses the following capacities: Light aircraft have 7-60 passengers, regional have 72-150 passengers, medium have 156-250 passengers, and large have 266-853 passengers. This is a new feature added to V2 of the game, and has it's own tab in the Aircraft menu.

General

The categorization for other uses is as follows: Light aircraft have 7-14 passengers, small have 15-59 passengers, regional have 60-149 passengers, medium have 150-249 passengers, large have 250-349 passengers, extra large have 350-499 passengers, and anything above that amount is a jumbo plane. If an airplane's speed is over 1,236 km/h, it will be categorized as Supersonic, no matter the capacity.

Applications

Turnaround Time

To find an airplane's turnaround time (extra time added to a route for cleaning, deplaning, etc.), divide the capacity by the number based on the category below:

  • Light, Small, and Regional: 3
  • Medium, Large, Extra Large, Jumbo, Supersonic: 2.5

Favorite Model Discount

For favoriting an aircraft model, you get a small discount in price that depends on the size. It is as follows:

  • Light: 20%
  • Regional: 15%
  • Small: 10%
  • Medium and Supersonic: 5%
  • Large: 4%
  • Extra Large: 3%
  • Jumbo: 2%
