Getting started

Example of a new user registering.

To get started in Airline club, you first need to make an account. To make an account, all you need is a username, email, password, and create a name for your airline. Be noted that you can't change any of these details later on!

Side note:

The email is just for password recovery purposes, the game should not spam you with emails.

Once you are happy with your details, click the Sign Up button. You will be greeted with the

World Map

page, and the game tutorials. They do a pretty good job in explaining the game or join the Airline Club discord, by clicking the button next to your "Logout" button & sign up. You can then ask any questions in the community-support channel.

Page after pressing the Sign Up button

We hope you enjoy your experience with the game!
