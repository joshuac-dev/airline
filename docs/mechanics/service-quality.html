Service Quality

Service quality is a customizable option in the

office

tab.

Adjusting

Service quality is changed over time. If you want to set service quality to a particular level, look next to "Airport Bases" and find "Target Service Quality". Press the gear icon and input a number from 0-100, then press the checkmark button (or if you accidentally deleted it, press the red X button to revert your changes).

Service quality affects satisfaction factor (or SF). A higher quality does not always translate into a higher satisfaction factor.

IFE

IFEs on JFK-LHR.

By going to the search tab and looking up a flight and clicking on it, There are little icons below the price which represents the IFE. Certain IFEs are unique to different kinds of planes (i.e. a

Cessna 421

is not going to have the same IFEs as a

Boeing 777-300ER

). You can

not

manually set specific IFEs.
