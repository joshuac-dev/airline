McDonnell Douglas

McDonnell Douglas was an American aerospace manufacturer and defense contractor formed from the merger of McDonnell Aircraft and Douglas Aircraft Company in 1967. The company was a major producer of commercial and military aircraft before merging with Boeing in 1997. McDonnell Douglas aircraft are known for their reliability and have been widely used by airlines around the world.
