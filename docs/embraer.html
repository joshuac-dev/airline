Embraer

Embraer is a Brazilian aerospace manufacturer that designs, manufactures, and sells commercial, military, executive, and agricultural aircraft. Embraer is one of the world's leading manufacturers of regional jets and has become a major player in the aviation industry, known for producing efficient and reliable aircraft for short to medium-haul routes.
