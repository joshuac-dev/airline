Boeing

Boeing is an American multinational aerospace corporation that designs, manufactures, and sells airplanes, rotorcraft, rockets, satellites, and telecommunications equipment worldwide. Boeing is one of the world's leading aircraft manufacturers, competing primarily with Airbus.
