Beechcraft Super King Air 200

The Beechcraft Super King Air 200 is a Turboprop plane.

In-Game

There are 38+ units in circulation.

Specs

Trivia

  • This is the longest produced turboprop plane, that has been in production since 1974.
  • The plane is pressurized, one of the two planes of this size that can (the other one being the Piaggio P.180 Avanti).
