Cessna

421 Caravan Citation X Game Stats Other Information Game Stats Other Information

The Cessna family comprises of light aircraft and a business jet.

In-Game

The Citation has 220+ units in circulation, most likely because of its range for its small size. It is then followed by the Caravan at 60+ units and then the 421 at 30+ units.

Trivia

  • At one point, there was a user who had bought (at least) 1,563 Cessna 421s. They would be banned later on for a different reason, and all of those 421s would be cleared by an administrator. It is unknown why the user bought all of those (that would be worth $859.650,000!).
  • The Citation family of aircraft includes 12 variants, the Citation X being the 7th to start production.
