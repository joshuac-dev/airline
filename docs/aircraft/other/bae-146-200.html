BAe 146-200

The BAe 146-200 is a four engine regional jet.

In-Game

The BAe 146-200 has over 240 units in circulation, a pretty decent amount for a standalone aircraft. Some theorize it is its cheapness that gives it this number, despite it not being the most fuel efficient for its size

Specs

Trivia

  • There is an improved version of the jet called the Avro RJ that is similar to the BAe series.
  • The reason for including four engines was to help takeoff with short runways, as well as runways that are in high elevations and high temperatures.
