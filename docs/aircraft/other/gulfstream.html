Gulfstream

The Gulfstream G650ER is a business jet produced by Gulfstream.

In-Game

The aircraft is extremely popular with over 550 units in circulation. Its range and size makes it a candidate for starting intercontinental routes.

Specs

Trivia

  • This aircraft, along with related G650, G700, and G800 variants can fly up to impressive speeds of Mach 0.925 (0.925 times the speed of sound, or around 1,103 km/h (686 mph)!).
