Dornier 328

The Dornier 328 family is a series of regional airliners.

In-Game

Both models have around 30 units in circulation.

Specs

Trivia

  • The -110 is not the first Dornier 320 designed (being the -100). This variant has improved range and weight limitations than the -100.
  • There were more planned variants of the 328JET until Dornier went bankrupt. Since then, several parties have tried to resume development but gave up eventually.
