Ilyushin Il-96

The Ilyushin Il-96 is a family of quad jet airliners made by Ilyushin.

In-Game

The -300 has a few orders, at around 35 units in circulation. On the other hand, the -400 has no orders.

Specs

Trivia

  • The aircraft family had a limited 21 year production run (1988-2009) of its passenger variants due to production constraints. Around 30 total were built, with their service in Russian and Cuban airlines.
  • The Il-96M and Il-96-400 are similar airframes, with the -400 being fitted with Russian-designed equipment, due to tensions between the country of supply.
