Concorde

Concorde is one of two commercial supersonic airliners to ever be flown. It was a joint venture between BAe and Sud Aviation.

In-Game

There are 14 units in circulation, all under Galactic Republic.

Trivia

  • There are 17 Concordes on display in various locations in France, the United Kingdom, United States of America, Germany, and even one in Barbados.
  • Along with Air France and British Airways, Braniff International Airways and Singapore Airlines operated a Concorde under their name.
