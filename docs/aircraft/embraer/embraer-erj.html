The Embraer ERJ family is a variety of regional aircraft.

In-Game

The Embraer EMB190 is the most popular of the family and is the only one that has lost its discounts at around 490 units in circulation. This is then followed by the EMB120 (~280 units), then the ERJ145/145XR (~210 units each), the EMB170-200 (~200units), then the ERJ140 (~190 units), the ERJ170 (~160 units), then the ERJ135 (~110 units), and lastly the ERJ175 (~50 units!).

Trivia

  • The oldest model in the family, the EMB120 Brasilia, started production in 1983 lasting to 2001.
  • The ERJ175 and EMB190 are the only models still currently in production (December 2021).
  • The ERJ145 has a military variant that utilizes radar for a few military operators.
  • The in-game EMB170-200 most likely corresponds to the E175, due to the official designation of the former is "ERJ 170-200 LR/STD"
