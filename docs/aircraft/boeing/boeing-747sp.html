Boeing 747SP

Pan Am
In-game

Airplane Family: Boeing 747

Related to: Airbus A340-600, Airbus A380, Boeing 777-300ER

Manufacturer: Boeing

The Boeing 747SP (for Special Performance) has the longest range of all the 747s.

This model is very popular in game since it is the fastest subsonic plane, being able to cancel out some of it's fuel inefficiency because fuel burn is calculated by flight time. This model is especially popular among members of Societe du Bumbum, with 4 of their 12 airlines on the top list in terms of number of the type owned.

Trivia

  • This variant of the 747 was requested by Pan Am to fly long routes from New York and the Middle East.
  • The plane didn't sell that well, and sold 45 units, four of which are still in service (Apr 2022).
