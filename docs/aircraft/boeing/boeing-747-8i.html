Boeing 747-8i

Airplane Family: Boeing 747

Related to: Airbus A340-600, Airbus A380, Boeing 777-300ER

Manufacturer: Boeing

The Boeing 747-8i (or Boeing 747-8 Intercontinental) is Boeing's most recent quad-engine aircraft.

Trivia

  • The 747-8F (cargo) was launched before the 747-8i (passenger), almost 8 months later.
  • The plane's initial design is the successor to VC-25A (or the transportation aircraft of the President of the United States), in which the US signed a 3.9 billion USD contract with Boeing to build them.
