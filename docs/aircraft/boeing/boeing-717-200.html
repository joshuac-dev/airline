Boeing 717-200

The Boeing 717-200 is regional plane with tail-mounted engines.

In-Game

Compared to its predecessors, the 717 has more orders than the MD-90, but is not as popular the MD-81 (717 has 20+ in circulation, MD-81 has 300+ in circulation).

Specs

Trivia

  • The 717 is a rebranded version of the MD-95, after Boeing merged with McDonnell Douglas in 1997.
  • Qantas became an accidental owner of the 717 through acquiring Impulse. They have grown to love the aircraft.
