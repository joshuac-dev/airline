The Boeing 747-400 is a technologically more advanced version of the

747-300

. In-game, the -400 has an extra 1046 km of range, and 67 less fuel burn, but adds an extra $60,000,000 to the cost.

Trivia

  • There are six other variants of the -400 (-400F, -400M, -400D, -400ER, -400ERF, and -400BCF/SF), as well as the Boeing 747 Large Cargo Freighter (Or the Boeing Dreamlifter).
  • The -400 is the most successful 747, selling 694 of the 1,562 (or ~45%) delivered aircraft of the family.
