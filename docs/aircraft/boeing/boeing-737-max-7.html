Boeing 737 MAX 7

Airplane Family: Boeing B737 MAX

Related to: Airbus A320neo Variants, Comac C919

The Boeing B737 MAX 7 is the smallest variant in the B737 MAX series.

Trivia

  • The B737-7 [ 1 ] is based of the B737-700 , like how the MAX 8 and 9 are based off the B737-800 and -900 respectively.
  • The wings and landing gear come from the -8, and has a longer fuselage than the -700.
