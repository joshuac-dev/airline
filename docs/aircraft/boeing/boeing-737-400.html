In-game

Airplane Family: Boeing 737 Classic

Related to: Airbus A320 Family, Embraer EMB190, McDonnell Douglas MD-80/90

Manufacturer: Airbus

The 737-400 is another elongated variant to the Classic family.

Trivia

  • The last -400s (and last 737 Classics) were delivered to CSA Czech Airlines [ 1 ] , which is no longer part of their fleet.
  • There are unofficial freighter versions, all converted by airlines and not produced by Boeing.
