Trivia

  • The 727 is the only trijet Boeing produced, and one of the two planes with a rear stair exit (the other being the B717 ). A hijacking in 1971 utilized the airstairs to parachute out of the plane,
  • A 727-200 participated in a controlled crash in 2012, to study safety and effects during and after a crash .
