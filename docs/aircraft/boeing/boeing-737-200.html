Related to: Airbus A320 Family, Embraer EMB190, McDonnell Douglas MD-80/90, Sukhoi Superjet 100

The 737-200 is the first variant of the 737, stretched out for more capacity.

Trivia

  • There are still 46 -200s in service as of July 2019, in countries with smaller airlines.
  • A modification lets planes land on runways that are unpaved, allowing it to land at some remote airports.

In-game

In Airlines Club, there are 47 -200s in circulation, with Nanchang-based carrier AirBlackout operating almost half, with 23 of the type. It is discounted in the game due to low demand.
