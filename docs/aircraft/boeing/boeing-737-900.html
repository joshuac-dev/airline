The Boeing 737-900 is the largest and longest variant of the 737 NG family.

Trivia

  • Despite having more room for more passengers, the -900 has the same fuel capacity and MTOW as the -800 , meaning it should have a smaller range than the -800.
  • The -900ER variant was much more popular than the -900, and had 10 times less orders than the -ER.
