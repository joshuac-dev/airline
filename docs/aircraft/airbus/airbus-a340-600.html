Airbus A340-600

Thai Airways
In-game

Airplane Family: Airbus A340

Related to: Boeing 777, McDonnell Douglas MD-11

Manufacturer: Airbus

The Airbus A340-600 has the highest capacity out of the A340 family.

Trivia

  • The original intent for the -600 was to replace some of the smaller B747 s.
  • There have been multiple advisories by Airbus to adjust cargo weight, in which airlines may sue them.
