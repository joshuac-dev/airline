Airbus A350-800

Airplane Family: Airbus A350

Related to: Airbus A330, Boeing B777, Boeing B787 Dreamliner

Manufacturer: Airbus

The Airbus A350-800 is the smallest in the A350 family.

Trivia

  • The -800 has actually never been built, as all orders have shifted to other A350s.
  • The design was intended to help the A330-200 on their routes.
