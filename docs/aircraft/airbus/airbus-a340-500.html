Airbus A340-500

Kingfisher
In-game

Airplane Family: Airbus A340

Related to: Boeing B777, McDonnell Douglas MD-11

Manufacturer: Airbus

The Airbus A340-500 is an ultra-long range aircraft, shortened from the

-600.

Trivia

  • The -500 was on one of the longest routes in the world, from Singapore (SIN) to New York (JFK), with a distance of 15,344 km, taking around 18 hours to complete.
  • This was the longest-ranged aircraft until the Airbus A350 XWB (ULR) came out.
