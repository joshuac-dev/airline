The Airbus A321neoLR is similar to the A321neo, but with extra range from extra fuel capacity.

Trivia

  • The neoLR is considered to be a good temporary replacement for the B757 s, which are getting old and need a replacement [ 1 ] .
  • The first neoLR was delivered to Arkia Israeli Airlines, in November 2018. They currently have two in service.
