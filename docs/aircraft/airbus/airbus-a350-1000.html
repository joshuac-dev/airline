Airbus A350-1000

Cathay Pacific
In-game

Airplane Family: Airbus A350

Related to: Airbus A330, Boeing B777, Boeing B787 Dreamliner

Manufacturer: Airbus

The Airbus A350-1000 is the largest plane in the A350 family.

Trivia

  • Some airlines were disappointed with the final design of the -1000, and canceled their orders for the aircraft.
  • Qantas chose the -1000 to perform non-stop "Kangaroo Routes" [ 1 ] [ 2 ] (Routes from Australia to locations in the Eastern Hemisphere).
