Airbus A300-600

Corporate
In-game

Airplane Family: Airbus A300/A310

Related to: Boeing 767, Lockheed L-1011, McDonnell Douglas DC-10

Manufacturer: Airbus

The A300-600 is a medium-range wide body airliner. The A300 family is the first two turbofan engine to be developed.

Trivia

  • The first A300 nearly didn't sell [ 1 ] due to American predominance in the market at the time.
  • The A3300-600ST (Also called the Airbus Beluga) was designed from the A300-600.
