The Airbus A350 family is a long-range, wide-bodied aircraft, and the newest family in Airbus, first starting design in 2004 and entering service in 2014.

In-Game

The A350-800 is the most ordered, at 560+ units, followed by the -1000 (360+), and then the -900 and -900ULR (150+ and 40+ respectively).

Trivia

  • The Airbus Corporate Jet version of the -900ULR can fly up to 20,000 km!
  • A planned regional variant was proposed, but there has been no new information about it.
