The A350-900ULR is the world's longest ranged commercial airliner.

Trivia

  • The Corporate Jet can fly up to 20,000 km, which is derived from the ULR with extra fuel capacity.
  • This plane also flew the longest route, Singapore to Newark, in 17 hours, 52 minutes, covering a distance of 15,353 km.
