Airbus A330-800neo

Kuwait Airways
In-game

Airplane Family: Airbus A330

Related to: Airbus A350, Boeing B787 Dreamliner

Manufacturer: Airbus

The A330-800neo is the newest airplane in the A330, first in service on the 29th of October, 2020.

Trivia

  • The fuselage is the same as the -200, but has a few more seats.
  • There is little demand for the -800, and may be replaced by the-200
