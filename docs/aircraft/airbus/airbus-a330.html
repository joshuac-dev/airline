Airbus A330

A330-200 A330-300 A330-800neo A330-900neo Game Stats Other Information

The Airbus A330 family is another plane derived from the

A300

. Like the

A320

, there are both a current engine option (ceo) and new engine options (neo).

In-Game

The A330-900neo has the highest amount of units in circulation, at 785+. It could be due to the capacity and fuel per pax that makes it so popular. The -300 and -800neo both have 175+ units, and the -200 has the least at 80+.
