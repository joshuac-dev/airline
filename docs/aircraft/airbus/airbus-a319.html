Trivia

  • In development, the A319 was called the A320M-7 , which stood for the "A320 minus seven fuselage frames" .
  • A corporate jet version of the A319 is built, which adds fuel capacity. There is only one of these currently in existence, with the registration ACJ319.
  • There is a military variant as well called the A319 MPA (Maritime Patrol Aircraft), being designed to compete with Boeing's similar concept off of the B737 .
