Airbus A310-300

Air Transat
In-game

Airplane Family: Airbus A300/A310

Related to: Airbus A300, Boeing 767 McDonnell Douglas DC-10

Manufacturer: Airbus

The Airbus A310-300 is almost similar

A310-200

, but adds extra range with its increased MTOW. This enables the plane to carry extra fuel without sacrificing passengers, thus improving the range.

Trivia

  • There is an -300 variant that can change between cargo and passengers by removing the seating.
  • A fuel distribution system controlled by a computer moves fuel to change the plane's center of gravity.
