The Airbus A320neo is the newer version of the Airbus A320ceo. The difference in engines is that the neo has CFM International LEAP-1A or P&W PW1100G, while the ceo has CFM International CFM56 engines.

Trivia

  • The new engines make the plane 15% more efficient, and increases the range by 500 km.
  • The first A320neo was delivered to Lufthansa in January of 2016. Since then, there have been 3,852 orders and 1,204 deliveries.
