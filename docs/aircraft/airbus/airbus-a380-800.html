Airbus A380-800

The A380-800 is the world's largest passenger airplane to date. The original intent of the plane was to challenge the

Boeing 747

, and to help clear up congestion at busy, international airports.

In-Game

The number of A380s in circulation is low compared to the total in circulation of 747 family, but has more orders than all aircraft in that family except for the 747SP and 747-8i.

Trivia

  • There was a planned version of the A380 that would be even bigger, seating around 900 passengers! This version never made it due to Airbus canceling production of the -800 due to the pandemic.
  • The plane never really replaced the 747, which was replaced with 767 s and 777s .
