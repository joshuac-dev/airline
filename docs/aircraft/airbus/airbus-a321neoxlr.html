Airbus A321neoXLR

Corporate
In-game

Airplane Family: Airbus A320

Related to: Boeing B737 MAX, Comac C919, Embraer E-Jet E2

Manufacturer: Airbus

The Airbus A321neoXLR is the newest A320 variant, and has the longest range in the family.

Trivia

  • There are differing views on the neoXLR, with some airlines placing orders immediately, and others questioning its usefulnesss.
  • There have been 450 orders for the neoXLR in April of 2020.
  • The in-game image for the XLR is the same as the LR , due to it not being drawn by Norebbo.
