Bombardier CS

CS100 CS300 Game Stats Other Information

The Bombardier CSeries (Or CS) are regional jets that have been rebranded into the Airbus A220.

In-Game

The CS100 is by far more popular than the larger vairent, the CS300, each having 1,860+ and 370+ units respectively.

Trivia

  • The CSeries has not, and will probably not, be renamed to the Airbus A220. One theory going around in the community is that the developer takes pride in Bombardier and will not update the names of this or the CRJ family.
  • The reason for the CS rebranding into an Airbus plane was brough on by a tariff by Boeing. More details here video by Wendover Productions.
