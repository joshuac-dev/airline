The Bombardier CRJ family is a popular regional jetliner.

In-Game

CRJs are one of the most recommended starting aircraft (along with their sister family, the

DHCs

), and were quickly removed of their Low Demand discount and became a small crisis

[

]

. The CRJ1000 is the most popular plane, at 580 units ordered. The CRJ700 is the second most ordered plane with ~300 units, and then the CRJ900. Between the CRJ100/200, the CRJ200 has a few more orders.

Comparing to similar sized

Embraers

, the CRJ is still more popular, although is slowly losing it's title to the ERJ series. The EMB190 has ~450 orders.

Trivia

  • Bombardier no longer sells or produces the CRJ series, as it has sold the program to Mitsubishi.
  • There are other variants in the CRJ family not in-game: the CRJ100SF/LR/SE, CRJ200PF/LR/SF, CRJ440 (smaller variant), CRJ500 (smaller CRJ700), Challenger 800/850 (the business jet version of the CRJ200), and a CRJ705 (a heavy business class cabin for the CRJ900).
