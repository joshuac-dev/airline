Comac C919

The Comac C919 is a medium-range airliner made by the Chinese company Comac.

In-Game

the C919 has around 200 units in circulation. All are under two airlines currently.

Specs

Trivia

  • Ryanair was to be a co-developer of the C919 but the deal was canceled on Comac's end.
  • If you look close enough, there are several parts that resemble several aircraft from Boeing and Airbus (the A320 , A350 , 787 , and 777-9 ).
