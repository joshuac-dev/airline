Ilyushin

Ilyushin is a Russian aircraft manufacturer and design bureau, founded by Sergey Vladimirovich Ilyushin in 1933. The company has a long history of producing both military and civilian aircraft, with particular expertise in long-range jet airliners. Ilyushin aircraft are known for their robust construction and have been widely used by airlines in Russia and other countries.
