Fokker

Fokker was a Dutch aircraft manufacturer that designed, manufactured, and sold civil and military aircraft. Known for producing reliable regional aircraft, Fokker played a significant role in aviation history before its bankruptcy in 1996. The company's aircraft, particularly the Fokker 50, 70, and 100, remain popular choices for regional routes in the game.
