The Bombardier DHC-8 family (or "dash 8") is a family of turboprop regional airliners.


In-Game

The most popular model th the DHC-8-400, followed by the -8-300 and Q400 at around 370, and then the -8-200, -8-100, and -6-400 (all three have less than 200 units in circulation each).

Trivia

  • The DHC-6 is called the Twin Otter, which could be from the DHC-3 Otter. That had a single, nose-mounted engine.
  • Bombardier made a Q version for the DHC-8-200, -300, and -400. These variants were quieter than its predicecors and competetors hence the "Q".
  • There are several companies who have aquired the program form Bombardier: Viking Air (DHC-6 series 400) in 2006 and Longview Aviation Capital Corporation/De Havilland Canada (DHC-8-100,-200,-300, and Q400) after 2019.
  • Viking Air has also purchased the rights to produce all DHC-1 to DHC-7 aircraft in 2006.
  • The in-game Q400 is really the Q400NextGen, which had a capacity increace in 2013 and 2016 to the maximum 90 seats we have today.
