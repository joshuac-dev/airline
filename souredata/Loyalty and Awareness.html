Loyalty and Awareness

Loyalty and awareness are two mechanics used to determine passenger loads and loyalists.

Awareness will only show in airports you fly to (the checkmark in the green circle), while you can have loyalty (the heart) in airports you don't fly to. To increase your loyalty, you can add more flights to the target airport, or make a campaign.

Campaigns

By going to the office, you can use delegates and money to start campaigns around an airport (or one of the airports in a city). Click the gear button next to "campaigns" section, then a plus. Type in the airport you want to make the campaign in, then select the range and the number of delegates.

Increasing the range decreases the awareness and loyalty gained, while increasing the delegates increases the weekly cost.

Losing Loyalty/Awareness

If you stop flying to a route (or someone else begins to take over your routes), your loyalty and awareness starts to fade away. There is a set amount it will decay by per week, but it may vary based on activity of other airlines.
