The Boeing 747 is a long-range, wide-body quad jet aircraft, and Boeing's most recognizable aircraft with its forward hump.

In-Game

Interestingly, the most popular model was the 747SP, followed by the 747-8i. Now, it's close, but the -8i is more popular by a few models.

Trivia

  • The 747s are the first wide-body aircraft.
  • The 747-400ER is really the 747-400 but with extra fuel tanks, and was only ordered by Qantas.
  • There is a 747-200 you can stay in as a hotel in Stockholm. It is located near Arlanda Airport, and has 35 rooms.
