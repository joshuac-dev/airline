The Boeing 767-200 is the most popular plane ordered, followed by the -400ER and -200ER.

Trivia

  • Originally intended for routes from the east and west coasts of the US, the 767 moved on to trans-oceanic routes and was the first twin jet-engine aircraft to do so with TWA.
