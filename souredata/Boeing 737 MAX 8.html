The Boeing B737 MAX 8 is the first MAX to be designed, and the plane type that caused the March 2019 groundings.

Trivia

  • There is a planned MAX 8-200, which would increase the capacity of the plane to 200 seats.
  • On the test flight, it has been noted that the plane had an "unusually far forward" center of gravity, maybe foreshadowing the cause of the crashes.
