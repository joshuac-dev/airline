The Boeing 737-800 is an elongated version of the 737-700.

Trivia

  • While the -800 replaced 737-400s and 727-200s, it also replaced the MD-80/90 following the merger with Mcdonnell Douglas.
  • The -800 is the most sold of the NG family, having 3,863 more orders than the second most sold (-700, at 1,128 units), and makes up more than half of the orders for the NG family.
