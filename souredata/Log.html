Log

The logs are the game's way of relaying warnings and messages pertaining to the airline.

Alerts

This section shows warnings related to you, such as warning you of a route closure due to low load factor. If the load factor remains below 50% for the remaining listed duration on that flight, said flight will be closed.

Logs

There are multiple types of alerts that can show up in this section.

  • Negotiation Discounts
  • Airport Rank Change
  • Flight Route
  • Self Note

Negotiation Discount

When you fail a negotiation, you get a certain discount for future negotiations, up to 10%. These discounts expire within 30 weeks of the failed negotiation and stack.

Airport Rank Change

When the rank at an airport changes, you get an alert showing how much reputation you gain or lose and which rank you are now in.

Flight Route

When other companies' flights close automatically due to low load factor, you get an alert saying which flight the airline was forced to stop.

Self Note

While not really an alert, you can add notes for yourself.
