The airbus A320 is the first plane in the A320 family. All variants in the family are either stretched or shortened versions of this plane.

Trivia

  • The A320 has nine different engine options (not counting the neo ones)
  • Some additional parts, such as winglets, were added to A320s after 2006. This was part of a project to make the plane more efficient.
