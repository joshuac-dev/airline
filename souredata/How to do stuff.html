How to do stuff

This is an extension of the FAQ that lists every possible action you can take on Airline Club. This tells you how to do things, but not what you should do, which belongs in the FAQ and the guide. This is a WIP article.

Routes

Make a route

You'll need to have a HQ airport and an airplane

  1. Click an airport you want to fly to.
  2. Click the "Plan Flight" button
  3. If you need to buy an airplane, select an airplane from the dropdown list, scroll down, and click the Buy button.
  4. Select the airplane icon
  5. Click the grey circles that pop up. 1 circle represents 1 flight per week. You can move your cursor over the circles to select more flights per week.
  6. Click the "Create" button
  7. If the route requires delegates, assign some delegates to help you negotiate the route with the green plus button.
  8. Click the "Negotiate" button.
  9. Click the button that appears under the negotiation window.

Build an HQ

  1. Select an airport
  2. Click "View Airport" button
  3. Click "Build Headquarters" button
