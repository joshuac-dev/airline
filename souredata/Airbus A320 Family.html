Airbus A320 Family

Were you looking for the planes?

The Airbus A320 is a narrow-body turbofan airliner, and is the most ordered commercial airplane to date [1], even more than the Boeing B737 series[2]. There are two versions: the current engine option (ceo) and new engine option (neo). The neo versions are supposedly more fuel efficient than the ceo.
