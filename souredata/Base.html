Base

A "Base" is needed at an airport to be able to make flights out of that airport.

Bases/HQ

The "HQ" is every airline's first base. Unlike a typical base, HQ's have an increased staff limit, and are a yellow color. On the other hand, bases are green with a lower amount of staff (see table in Upgrading).

Going over the staff limit will incur extra costs in the following formula[1]: 

(staff needed-staff capacity)*(50000 + airport's income)/52*10


Delegates Cost

HQ/Bases require delegates assigned to the country of the airport to maintain.

Both HQ and Base require 1 additional delegate every 2 levels. HQ get the first level free, bases require 1 delegate at level 1. [2]
