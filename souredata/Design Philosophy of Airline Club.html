Design Philosophy of Airline Club

Airline Club is almost entirely influenced by Air Canada/the dev's wishes regarding its development. His background in gaming has shaped AC's genre and UI. Here, we try to explain his thoughts about AC.

Philosophy

Air Canada grew up playing old-school games. These games mainly focused on finding out game mechanics by yourself and being harsh on failing players. Airline Club reflects that by having a minimal tutorial.
