Liveries

Liveries are assortments of colors, graphics, and identifiers (text) on an aircraft that are painted on an aircraft.

The game allows you to import a livery to represent your airline in the office menu.

How to make a livery

  1. Download the necessary tools and materials: a photo editor (GIMP works pretty well, v 2.10 & is free, paint.net, Adobe Photoshop, ), and a plane base (you can use the in-game images or get one from here or an extensive list Airline livery bases)
  2. Open the image and add your design and/or logo.
  3. Save as a .png file.
  4. Go to the office in-game and look for the Airplane Livery section on the right (under Headquarters) and press the image with a plus.
  5. Press upload to select your livery, and wait for it to load
  6. Done!

Designs

You don't have to be overly creative to make a good looking livery, just have a play after watching the either two linked tutorials (or many others online), it can be very satisfying to make your own, or if you aren't up for it, get on AC'd discord/livery requests & ask for one of the talented members there if they can make one & they will happily do one for you.
