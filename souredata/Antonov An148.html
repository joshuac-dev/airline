Trivia

  • The plane was grounded in 2018 following a crash that resulted in 71 fatalities.
  • For some time, the plane was also produced by Voronezh Aircraft Production Association, a Russian production plant. However, due to tense relations between them and Ukraine, the plant has suspended production.
  • There is a larger variant called the An158.
